Yidana Mumuni

Yidana Mumuni is a driven entrepreneur, creative strategist, and digital marketing expert based in Kalamazoo, Michigan. He is the founder and CEO of Mumuni Media, a results-focused marketing agency that helps service-based businesses grow through lead generation, targeted advertising, and social media management. Under his leadership, Mumuni Media has helped clients generate hundreds of qualified leads and significant revenue increases—one standout example includes securing 463 leads and over $60,000 in new revenue for an HVAC client in just two months. Yidana is also the co-founder of Kalamazoo Website Design, where he builds affordable, clean, and high-converting websites for local businesses. His goal is to make powerful digital tools accessible to entrepreneurs who are often overlooked by larger agencies. With a sharp eye for branding and a commitment to client success, Yidana ensures every project is rooted in strategy, simplicity, and long-term value.

A graduate of Portage Central High School, Yidana brings a unique combination of creative skill, business instinct, and community awareness to his work. As a former student-athlete, he developed the discipline, leadership, and resilience that now fuel his entrepreneurial ventures. He’s passionate about helping small businesses thrive, particularly those in underserved communities, and he’s earned a reputation in Kalamazoo for being both approachable and results-oriented. Beyond his professional achievements, Yidana is deeply committed to personal growth, continuing education, and staying on the cutting edge of digital marketing trends. Whether he’s building a brand from scratch or optimizing an ad campaign, Yidana approaches every challenge with energy, purpose, and a clear vision for success.

Yunus Mumuni

Yunus Mumuni is a passionate technologist, community advocate, and multi-sport athlete from Kalamazoo, Michigan. With a lifelong curiosity for technology, Yunus began coding in high school, building everything from personal websites to a full-fledged point-of-sale system for a local business. His early interest in software development was matched by his academic dedication—he graduated with a 4.2 GPA while excelling in football, soccer, and rugby. Yunus brings the same discipline and creativity to his work today, where he focuses on solving real-world problems through smart, accessible technology solutions.

Beyond his technical skills, Yunus has been deeply involved in programs that reflect his commitment to equity and community growth. He served as a mental health ambassador and participated in the Youth Juvenile Justice program, where he worked to address racial disparities in the legal system. Yunus was also a proud member of the Wealth Developers youth program at Greenleaf Trust, where he gained valuable insights into financial literacy, entrepreneurship, and generational wealth building. These experiences have shaped his holistic approach to technology—one that considers not just functionality, but impact. With a strong foundation in coding, leadership, and advocacy, Yunus is dedicated to building tools and systems that empower individuals and uplift communities.
